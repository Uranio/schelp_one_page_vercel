How it works

From a spark, an episode. Made for you.

01

Start from a spark

Write an idea, speak into the mic, or send a photo. Schelp reads the context, pulls the right sources live, and gives you a well-built episode.

  • Prompt
  • Voice
  • Photo
1.1

Quick mode

One sentence, that's it. Schelp picks the sources, the voice, the tone and the length. Ready in minutes.

1.2

Advanced mode

Want more control? Pick a narrative style and a cover style, then move to the next step.

1.3

Pick the sources

Let the AI search the live web, or hand-pick the sites Schelp should pull from.

1.4

Length, depth, voice

From 2 to 15 minutes. Light or deep research. A hundred voices to choose from — then hit "Create podcast".

02

Tell Schelp who you are

Three quick steps after sign-up: what excites you, when you have ten minutes to listen, and what goal you want to reach. From that moment on, every episode is tailored to you.

  • Interests
  • Habits
  • Goals
2.1

Pick your interests

Tech, news, cinema, food, science — give Schelp the topics that light you up and the app starts weaving your favourites together.

2.2

Set your habits

When do you have ten minutes? The morning commute, the gym at 6 pm, before bed. Schelp slips episodes into the windows you actually have.

2.3

Set a goal

Learn a language, stay current in a field, dig deep into a topic. An intention that becomes a series of episodes planned for months.

03

Slip it into the cracks of your day

Tell me when you have time: the commute, the gym, the break, before bed. Schelp plans episodes into those windows and waits for you there.

  • Commute
  • Gym
  • Break
  • Before bed
04

Open the inside story

Every episode has its page: the full script, the cited sources, the generation parameters, the length, the voice. See what Schelp read and where it got the news.

  • Script
  • Sources
  • Parameters
05

And around it, a community

Schelp isn't just your podcasts. Discover other people's, follow who inspires you and organise everything into playlists.

  • Discover
  • Following
  • Series
5.1

Discover new episodes

Picks of the week, podcasts on your interests, the community's series. Search people, topics and series from a single screen.

5.2

Follow who inspires you

Profiles, followers and following: find the creators you love, see who your friends follow, and never miss their new episodes.

5.3

Open a series

Series published in Discover are multi-episode journeys: open one and listen start to finish, with your progress always saved.
