What Schelp does

One prompt, many podcasts.

Type a single sentence, tweak a few knobs — length, vibe, hosts — and Schelp spins up an entire shelf of fresh episodes around it. Pick one and press play.

Pick a goal, get a series.

"Catch up on AI every morning." "Understand the markets in 30 days." Schelp turns the goal into episodes and drops them into the cracks of your real day — commute, run, dinner, bed.

Even from a photo.

Drop in an image — a book cover, a screenshot, a place you visited — and Schelp builds an episode around it. Total freedom on what becomes audio.
